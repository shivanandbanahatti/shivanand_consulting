How engagements run

  1. Discovery — 30–45 minutes to understand context, urgency, and constraints.
  2. Written scope — deliverables, assumptions, timeline, and price (or sprint budget).
  3. Delivery — weekly checkpoints, shared task list, and documented decisions.
  4. Handover — training, admin playbook, and optional support window.

Frequently asked

Do you work remotely across India?

Yes. Discovery and most delivery are remote; on-site workshops can be scheduled when needed (Bangalore base).

Cloud or self-hosted ERPNext?

Both. I help you choose based on compliance, integrations, and your internal IT capacity.

What if our customisations block upgrades?

The audit maps debt and proposes a safe refactor path — often moving logic into supported patterns (DocTypes, hooks, APIs) before version jumps.

Do you subcontract delivery?

Core architecture and ERPNext work are led directly. Larger programmes may use a small trusted bench with clear ownership.
