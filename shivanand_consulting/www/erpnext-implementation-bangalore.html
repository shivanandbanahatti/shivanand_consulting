Who this is for

Growing FMCG distributors, manufacturers, and multi-branch operators in Bangalore who have outgrown spreadsheets or a partial ERP rollout. You may be comparing partners, rescuing a stalled implementation, or moving from Tally to a single source of truth.

What a Bangalore ERPNext implementation includes

  • Discovery & blueprint — process mapping, module selection, data migration plan, and written scope with timeline.
  • Core modules — accounting, inventory, sales, purchase, manufacturing or distribution workflows as needed.
  • India compliance — GST setup, tax templates, and operational reports finance can trust.
  • Customisation — Frappe DocTypes, server scripts, and approvals aligned to how your team actually works.
  • Integrations — Razorpay, logistics, CRM, or internal APIs so ERPNext is not an island.
  • Go-live & hypercare — UAT, cutover support, and training for power users and leadership MIS.

On-site in Bengaluru or remote

Workshops and go-live support can be on-site in Bangalore. For distributed teams, discovery and delivery are often hybrid — with clear async checkpoints and documented decisions. The same rigour applies whether you are in Whitefield, Peenya, or multi-city.

Frequently asked questions
